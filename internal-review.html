The story in one paragraph

Your Home (NJ) campaign is performing well — it sits near the top of the page on nearly every lead auction, converts 75% more leads into sales than Auto, and delivers a 24% lower cost per sale. Your Auto (NJ) campaign is the opposite story: the bids are positioned below the New Jersey market, so you're buried in the middle of the results page and reaching only a fraction of the available consumers. The good news is that Home proves what's possible at your agency — and with a measured bid adjustment on Auto, we expect Auto's volume to roughly triple at a comparable cost-per-sale.
